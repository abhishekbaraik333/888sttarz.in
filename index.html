Types of Bets

888Starz betting platform provides all the familiar types of bets and even more. Below, you can see some of them:

  • Single. It is a common type of bet where you make your prediction on a single event.
  • Accumulator. The accumulator bet is consistently one of the most popular forms of betting for punters because of the excitement of multiple selections combining their odds for an opportunity of enhanced winnings.
  • Chain bet. A chain is a type of bet that includes elements of a parlay and a system.
  • Multybet. Multi bet is a bet type that allows bettors to bet multiple times while using a single bet slip.
  • Conditional bet. A Conditional Bet is a placed wager that becomes activated only if certain criteria are met. A conditional bet allows you to set minimum requirements, or conditions, that must be satisfied for the bet to take place, or for the bet to continue.
  • Patent. A Patent bet is a form of multiple methods of betting wherein seven bets are placed on three different selections.
  • System bet. A system bet is a type of bet where you only need a few legs or selections of your bet to land for you to win. It’s different from an accumulator, for example, which requires all your selections to return spot-on for you to win.
